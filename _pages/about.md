👋 I am **Kyoungjoon (Dale) Ha**, an RTL designer for high-speed SerDes interconnect PHYs, including UCIe and MIPI D-PHY/C-PHY,
at **Samsung Electronics**. Before joining Samsung, I conducted a mixed-signal circuit research 🔬 on transistor-level PLLs,
including ADPLL and ILPLL. I am fluent across the analog/digital boundary, from transistor-level circuits to RTL ⚡.

📄 For a more detailed record of my work, please find my [CV](/files/kjha_cv.pdf).
